## CRITERION 1: Customer Understanding & Market Access (TARGET: 9/9 — TIEBREAKER #1)

### The Problem

**University students across Europe face a massive employability gap:**
- Entry-level IT positions = only **0.8%** of the Polish job market (justjoin.it, 2025)
- **55%** of Polish tech companies can't find candidates with the right skills
- Students graduate with theory but **zero industry certifications**
- IT certifications cost 500-2000 PLN each — students can't afford trial-and-error
- No guidance on WHICH certifications matter for WHICH career path

### Customer Segments

| Segment | Size | Pain Point |
|---------|------|------------|
| **Primary: University students** | 1.2M in Poland, 20M+ in EU | No clarity on career path, waste time/money on wrong certifications |
| **Secondary: University administrators** | ~400 universities in Poland, 4,000+ in EU | Need to improve graduate employability, reduce dropout |
| **Tertiary: Certification providers** (AWS, Google, CompTIA) | Global | Need student distribution channel |
| **Launch partner: WSB Merito / Grupa Merito** | 100K+ students, 14 institutions, 11 cities | Largest private university group in Poland |

## CRITERION 3: Business Model (TARGET: 9/9 — TIEBREAKER #2)

### Vision: University-Agnostic SaaS Platform

Clairy is NOT a WSB-only tool. It's a white-label SaaS platform any university can deploy.

### Revenue Streams

**1. University Licensing (PRIMARY — B2B)**
- Per-campus license: 2,000-5,000 PLN/month
- White-label portal, analytics dashboard, custom roadmaps
- Poland: 400 universities. EU: 4,000+ universities

**2. Freemium Student Access (B2C)**
- Free: onboarding + 2 lessons per skill
- Premium (29 PLN/month): full library, advanced challenges, personalized coach
- Target: 5% conversion of free users

**3. Certification Partner Program (B2B)**
- AWS, Google, Oracle, CompTIA pay for featured placement
- Affiliate commission on exam vouchers
- Sponsored lesson content

**4. Job Board (B2B)**
- Employers pay to reach cert-prepared students
- Premium listings: 500-2,000 PLN/month

### Financial Projection

|---|---|---|---|
| Revenue | 50K PLN | 1.5M PLN | 6M PLN |

| Net | -150K PLN | +700K PLN | +3.5M PLN |
| Users | 5,000 | 50,000 | 300,000 |
| Universities | 2 (pilot) | 10 | 40 |

### Market Sizing

- **TAM:** 4,000+ EU universities × 5,000 PLN/month = 240M PLN/year
- **SAM:** 400 Polish universities = 24M PLN/year

### Go-to-Market

**Phase 1 (0-6m):** WSB Merito pilot → case study with real data
