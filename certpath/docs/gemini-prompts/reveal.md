Look at Home.jsx and Dashboard_gemini.jsx — that's our design language. Now look at Reveal.jsx.

Rewrite it completely. Keep the loading-then-reveal two-phase flow, keep the data (field info, certs, jobs, salary range, stages), but reimagine how it all comes together visually. This is the page that makes someone go "wow, this is built for me." Make it feel that way.
