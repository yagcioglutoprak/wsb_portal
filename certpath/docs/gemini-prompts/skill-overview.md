# Redesign: SkillOverview.jsx

Look at Home.jsx and Dashboard_gemini.jsx — that's our design language. Now look at SkillOverview.jsx.

This page shows a single skill with its list of lessons — numbered, with progress bars, lock states, and start/continue/review buttons. It's the page you land on from the dashboard skill path.

Right now it's the most plain page in the whole app. Rewrite it from scratch. Look at how the "Your Skills" section and the "Continue Learning" card work on Dashboard_gemini — this page should feel like a natural deep-dive from there.

Make the lesson list feel like a progression — a journey with clear visual milestones. Locked lessons should feel locked but still exciting. Completed lessons should feel satisfying. The active lesson should pull you in. The skill badge at the top should feel earned when completed.

Keep all existing functionality: unlock logic, progress tracking, routing. Output the complete updated file.
